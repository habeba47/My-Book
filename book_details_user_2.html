Book Details

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

Author: J. K. Rowling

Published: 1998

Pages: 251

Description:The second novel in the series follows Harry's second year at Hogwarts, during which a series of messages on the walls warn of the "Chamber of Secrets" being opened. Harry, Ron, and Hermione investigate the mysterious attacks happening around the school. They discover the chamber was opened before, fifty years earlier, when Hagrid was expelled from Hogwarts.
