Book Details

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire

Author: J. K. Rowling

Published: 2000

Pages: 636

Description:During Harry's fourth year, Hogwarts hosts the Triwizard Tournament, a dangerous magical competition between three schools. Despite being underage, Harry is mysteriously selected as a fourth champion, leading to a deadly confrontation with Lord Voldemort. The book introduces other wizarding schools, Beauxbatons and Durmstrang, and their champion students.
